Date and pricing

This year's edition will take place from the 13th till the 15th of May 2016. You can sign up for this spectacular event now for a maximum of €7,50.

The LAN-party will start on Friday at 18:00. This may be changed at a later date but we'll keep you informed if it does! On saturday and sunday we will start at 10:00. On friday and saturday we will continue until 03:00, and on sunday we'll stop at 15:00.

Register

There are only limited spots available, so be quick to register!

When you plan to attend the LAN, don't forget to bring the following items:

  • Signed copy of the regulations
  • Your computer
  • LAN cable(s)
  • WiFi adapter (only eduroam is available for internet)
  • Headphones (speakers are not allowed)
  • A power strip to power your hardware
  • Other cables to connect your devices
  • Your mouse and keyboard
  • A mouse pad
  • Your games (pre-install Steam games)
  • OS install disk
